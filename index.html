About

Cubby is a web app that is made by people in the market to buy houses for people to buy houses. It started with the idea to be able to track houses that you have visited and find realtors in your area. We will set you up with a realtor and get you the asking prices for houses. You will be able to visit the houses and take pictures leave personal notes for yourself and record the pros and cons of owning the house in real time.
